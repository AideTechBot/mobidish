About Us

Mobi-Dish is a Canadian organization aimed at helping food insecure Canadians while providing a service that does not function as a food bank.

Fresh food is delivered directly to the homes, eliminating the stigma that surrounds food banks. If you believe that you'd be a suitable candidate for Mobi-Dish, we encourage you to apply.

Eliminate the stigma around food banks

Many Canadians who live in food insecurity don't use food banks. The inherent social stigma that surrounds food banks prevent 21.3% of food insecure Canadians from using them.

  • Source: Food Banks Canada
